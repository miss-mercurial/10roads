Programming

Ever since gymnasiet I have tried coding for fun. At DTU I chose courses where I could get better at coding, like introduction to coding in C, Java and Matlab, FEM heavy and Advanced Matlab Programming. In my Bachelor Project i made analytical calculations on 3D printed support structures using Matlab, and in my Master Thesis I implemented the finite element method in Matlab, to make FEM calculations on granular materials.

It has always fascinated me, how you can create something from nothing by writing text on the computer.

Throughout my career I have coded sheets, mainly VBA in Excel. Small programs to make time registration easier, and updated and improved larger programs for FEM calculations. I have also learned Pro Program, to make automated responsive parametric assemblies in Creo.

Now I am following HarvardX CS50x: CS50's Introduction to Computer Science to get better at programming and web development.
